Built for Queensland

Every workflow. Handled.

Not a generic platform with a QLD settings toggle — every process is mapped to the RTRA Act 2008.

🤖

VA-Powered Automation

Maintenance approvals, arrears notifications, lease renewal drafts and entry notices — handled automatically so your team focuses on what matters.

⚖️

RTRA Act Built-In

Form 11s, Form 12s, s.281 arrears timelines, s.192 entry notices — every notice period and form number mapped directly to Queensland legislation.

📊

Owner Reports in 30 Seconds

Auto-generated statements, maintenance summaries, rent history and disbursement records — ready to send without touching a template.

🛡️

Compliance Tracking

Smoke alarms, pool safety, gas, electrical and water compliance audited automatically. Nothing slips through.

🔄

Lease Renewal Workflows

CMA creation, tenant intention requests, owner approvals, document drafting and signing — the full renewal cycle managed end-to-end.

💰

Trust Accounting

Owner disbursements, tenant funds, bill processing, bank reconciliation — fully transparent trust management built into the platform.
