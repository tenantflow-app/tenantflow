How it works

Your AI assistant, always on.

Tenantflow's AI reads, classifies, and acts on tenant communications — so your inbox stops being the bottleneck.

1

Tenant emails arrive

Maintenance requests, rent queries, lease questions — they all land in Tenantflow's unified inbox, synced live from Gmail or Outlook.

2

AI classifies and triages

Claude AI reads each message, identifies the issue type, checks urgency against your portfolio data, and prepares the appropriate response and action.

3

You approve in one click

The AI drafts the reply, pre-fills the relevant form, and queues the action. You review and approve — or let it auto-handle routine items.

4

Everything is documented

Every action, reply, and form is logged to the property record with timestamps — creating an audit trail that protects you if anything ever goes to NCAT.

Built from the ground up for Queensland law.

Tenantflow isn't a generic platform with an "Australian settings" toggle. Every notice, timeline, and obligation is mapped to the Residential Tenancies and Rooming Accommodation Act 2008.

Correct Form 11 / Form 12 / NCAT timelines built in
Entry notice periods (24hr min / 48hr routine) enforced automatically
Smoke alarm compliance tracked under QLD legislation
Form 18a agreements with all required disclosures

CPD hours monitoring for property managers
Breach Notice
Form 11 — Notice to remedy breach. AI detects arrears at Day 2, prepares notice at Day 7 with correct RTA wording.
✓ RTA s.281
Entry Notice
Form 9 — Entry notice. Minimum 24-hr notice for urgent repairs, 48-hr for routine entry. Correct grounds pre-selected.
✓ RTA s.192
Termination Notice
Form 12 — Notice to leave. Correct notice periods by ground: 2 days (non-payment), 14 days (serious breach), 2 months (end of tenancy).
✓ RTA s.290
Tenancy Agreement
Form 18a — Generated from your property data, includes all required special conditions, rent payment terms, and bond disclosures.
✓ RTA s.55
Compliance & risk

Never be caught out again.

Tenantflow tracks every compliance obligation across your entire portfolio and alerts you before deadlines — not after.

✓
Smoke Alarm Compliance
Annual inspection tracking, contractor scheduling, and compliance certificates logged per property under Queensland Fire and Emergency Services requirements.
✓
Pool Safety Certificates
Certificate expiry tracking with 3-month, 1-month, and 2-week alerts. Auto-generates the required tenant notification documents on renewal.
✓
CPD Hour Tracking
Monitors Continuing Professional Development hours for each licensed property manager. Alerts when hours are running low before the annual renewal date.
✓
Maintenance Response Times
Tracks emergency vs routine maintenance response deadlines per RTA requirements. Escalates unresolved issues automatically to avoid liability exposure.
✓
Bond Lodgement Tracking
Confirms RTA bond lodgement within the mandatory 10-day window. Flags any outstanding lodgements with the correct corrective action steps.
✓
Full Audit Trail
Every action, notice, and communication is timestamped and stored. One-click NCAT bundle export compiles everything a tribunal needs in chronological order.
